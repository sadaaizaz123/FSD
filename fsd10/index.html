About CSE AIML

CSE AIML stands for Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. It is a four-year engineering degree that combines the core foundational subjects of Computer Science with advanced topics and practical training in AI and Machine Learning. This specialized program aims to equip students with the skills to develop intelligent systems and data-driven applications, focusing on areas like data science, deep learning, robotics, and natural language processing
